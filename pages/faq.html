Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Engibrief and our e-books.

What is Engibrief?

Engibrief is a platform that provides concise, exam-focused engineering e-books designed to help students revise faster and score better.

Do I need to create an account to browse e-books?

No. You can browse all available e-books without signing up. However, you must create an account to purchase and download e-books.

How do I purchase an e-book?

Simply log in or sign up, click on Buy Now, complete the payment, and the e-book will be available for download instantly.

What payment methods are supported?

We use Razorpay for secure payments. You can pay using UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and net banking.

Will I get lifetime access to my purchased e-books?

Yes. Once purchased, you will have lifetime access to your e-books from your account dashboard.

Can I download the e-books multiple times?

Yes. You can download your purchased e-books any number of times from your account, on any device.

Are refunds available?

Due to the digital nature of the content, refunds are generally not provided. Please refer to our Refund Policy for details.

How can I contact support?

If you have any issues or questions, please visit the Support page and contact us.
